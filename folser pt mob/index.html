Our Coffee Story

Welcome to Amurg Café, nestled in the heart of Club Napoca. Our café offers a warm and inviting atmosphere where the charm of twilight meets the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee.

At Amurg, we pride ourselves on serving an exquisite selection of coffee and teas, paired with delectable pastries and light bites. Whether you're looking for a cozy corner to unwind, a stylish spot to catch up with friends, or a peaceful workspace, Amurg Café provides the perfect setting.

Join us and let the serene ambiance of dusk enhance your café experience.
